History

Whitewater Rafting Adventure Rush was founded in 2013 by a group of nature enthusiasts who wanted to create a platform for an unforgettable adventure involving rafting in the most exotic South African rivers with breathtaking nature scenes. Whitewater Rafting Adventure Rush was one of the first prominent Rafting services in the Southern African religion and it holds it title of 'Best Rafting service' in South Africa.

Our company has branched into many adventure offers to enhance many activities for adventurers. We have grown and expanded to three more Southern African countries offering the same great service and thrilling rafting adventures into some of the most beautiful rivers offered in the Southern African religion.
